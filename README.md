# Azen AI
A Web-Based Community Command Center for Flood Preparedness & Response

Azen AI is a web-based disaster-response platform designed to support communities during flood events. It provides real-time situational awareness through verified alerts, hazard maps, and predictive models. Integrated with Google Gemini 2.5 Flash AI, the system delivers intelligent, location-aware guidance for residents and responders. A built-in Emergency Offline Mode ensures critical information remains accessible even without internet connectivity.

---

## Key Capabilities
- Real-time flood and weather alerts  
- Interactive command center dashboard  
- AI-powered safety guidance  
- On-ground validation through user reports  
- Predictive water level forecasting  
- Offline access to essential safety resources  

---

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Assistance
- Provides real-time safety guidance using Google Gemini 2.5 Flash AI  
- Answers questions, gives instructions, and assists with reporting  
- Generates location-aware and risk-sensitive recommendations  

---

### 2. Official Alerts Integration
- Pulls verified disaster information from the Google Crisis Response API  
- Ensures accuracy of flood alerts, weather updates, and hazard warnings  
- Reduces misinformation during critical events  

---

### 3. Command Center Dashboard
- Displays real-time risk levels and official alerts  
- Shows live forecasts, hazard maps, and local community reports  
- Serves as the central monitoring hub for responders and residents  

---

### 4. AI Water Level Forecast
- Predicts upcoming flood levels using historical and real-time data  
- Provides confidence scores for each prediction  
- Helps users make informed decisions based on likelihood and severity  

---

### 5. Map Visualization
- Interactive map with color-coded indicators:  
  - Red – Flooded areas  
  - Yellow – Potential risk zones  
  - Green – Safe locations  
- Helps users identify safe routes and evacuation paths quickly  

---

### 6. Emergency Offline Mode
- Allows access to essential information even without internet  
- Includes emergency contacts, instructions, and safety protocols  
- Ensures platform usability during outages or network failures  

---

## Community-Driven Insights
User-submitted reports enhance the platform’s Community Mitigation Protocols by enabling:
- More accurate ground-level validation  
- Tailored, community-specific safety recommendations  
- Continuous improvement of local response strategies
